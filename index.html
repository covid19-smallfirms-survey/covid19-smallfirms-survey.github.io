What?

The objective of our survey is to gather information about the effects of the current COVID-19 public health crisis on small businesses in the United States and Latin America. A second objective is to understand whether business owners are aware of the government relief policies aimed at them, and what other policies they think are necessary in this context.

This is an initiative of a group of researchers affiliated with Princeton University and Yale University. Our team is based out of the United States, the Dominican Republic, and Chile. We will post regular updates of the progress of our survey and some preliminary results in this website. If you have further questions about the project you can contact Christopher Neilson at cneilson@princeton.edu
